What if the images we generate aren't just creative outputs—but reflections of hidden systems at play?

BIAS IN FOCUS is an independent, research-driven archive that examines how generative AI distorts visual storytelling. Through 200 crowdsourced AI-generated images, each tagged across 25 different biases, this interactive platform reveals the subtle (and not-so-subtle) ways algorithms influence what—and who—we see.These visuals aren't random. They're organized through five key parameters, offering a framework for deeper analysis and critical reflection. From reinforcing stereotypes to erasing nuance, the biases on display challenge the assumption that AI-generated content is neutral. Created with a design-first approach, the project invites creatives, researchers, and the curious to interrogate the aesthetics of AI—and rethink what it means to create in the age of automation.
